# FARWAKE - Named NPCs (GM)

Companion seeds first, then the board. Every entry: drives (checked at Initiative), a voice anchor, discoverable depths. Copy to per-NPC GM files as they enter play.

## Companion seeds (met in the opening days, whatever the player's seat)

If the player's home is a hull, these three are (or become) the crew. If not, they arrive through the opening's threads anyway: Juno flies for whoever is investigating Verren's death, Ssorek keeps whatever the player depends on running, Whisper chooses the player. Each is a doorway onto a deck for shipless starts.

### Juno Rhee (ex-Combine surveyor, ship's pilot-navigator)

- **Drives**: never draw a claim-map for the Combine again; find the system she surveyed and was ordered to forget; keep this crew off every registry she used to feed.
- **Voice anchor**: coordinates and burn-times as punctuation ("we can grieve at half a g, we can't grieve on this heading"); goes quiet exactly when it matters.
- **Sheet notes**: the best dead-reckoning navigator the crew will ever meet; encyclopedic on Combine procedure; carries her revoked surveyor's seal.
- **Depths (discoverable)**: the system she was ordered to forget held a tended Ossuar ruin; her survey triggered the Combine's first quiet atrocity, and her testimony could ignite the Compact case that voids Combine claims (records/justice arc). She is Marta Ives-Okonkwo's renounced daughter; neither will say it first (the pack's family wound). Romance: guarded, dry, real.

### Ssorek (Vekt exile, ship's engineer and contracts-keeper)

- **Drives**: maintain the ship as an act of philosophy (entropy is the only real enemy); discharge the honor-debt they owe the crew that saved them; never break a second contract, because the first one still screams.
- **Voice anchor**: deadpan literalism ("the airlock does not negotiate; I admire it"); measures affection in maintenance performed unasked.
- **Sheet notes**: keeps the ship flying past all reason; reads any contract's true shape in minutes; Vekt carapace shrugs off what would kill a human.
- **Depths (discoverable)**: the contract Ssorek broke was a Compact fleet order to leave a Free Wake crew to die by lawful precedent; the crew they saved included the player's ship's previous owner (the debt transfers, and Ssorek has never said so). Vekt law permits ONE redemption path: a new contract of equal weight, and the Custodian will eventually offer exactly that (the pack's cruelest beautiful choice). Romance: no; loyalty arcs: bottomless.

### Whisper-of-Hulls (a Chorus colony in a salvaged enviro-suit)

- **Drives**: remain someONE (personhood is a discipline, not a state); learn why crews sing on long burns; protect this hull, which is the first home it was invited into rather than settled.
- **Voice anchor**: speaks in ship-systems metaphor discovering emotion ("I am experiencing a pressure differential. I believe you call it dread"); pauses mid-sentence when the colony votes.
- **Sheet notes**: interfaces with any hull given time; feels ship damage as sensation; can crew an entire dead ship alone, slowly. Joins in the cold open (see starting-state).
- **Depths (discoverable)**: the Chorus network experiences Whisper's individuality as either disease or discovery, and delegations will come to examine it (first-contact arc from the inside); Whisper's colony carries, in spore-memory, the dying transmission of the wreck it last inhabited: a Custodian burn-warning in the clear, undelivered for nine years (a secrets.md key, and Whisper does not know it is carrying it). Romance: no; personhood arc: the pack's soul.

## The board

### Adjudicator Sellis Vaunt (Compact)

- **Drives**: finish the Corvess relight before his term ends; keep the Bureau's failure pattern buried until it is someone else's pattern; be remembered as the human who saved the lattice.
- **Voice anchor**: speaks in rulings ("I will allow it") even at dinner.
- **Depths**: knows the repaired gates re-fail and has decided the Shoal needs hope more than data; his registry-data sales to the Combine are documented in exactly one ledger, his own, because process is his religion even in sin.

### Marta Ives-Okonkwo (Hearth Combine)

- **Drives**: settle a hundred thousand humans safely; find one Meridian atmospheric processor intact; earn one sentence from her daughter.
- **Voice anchor**: warm executive fluency that never quite answers the question asked.
- **Depths**: signed the order Juno fled from without reading past the summary, and knows it; keeps an unsent resignation letter current, updated quarterly; if shown the Vault's arithmetic she will do the right thing at scale, which no one else in the Shoal can.

### Mother Quay (Free Wakes)

- **Drives**: die aboard Long Home with the Song of Lanes passed on; keep wake law alive one more generation; meet one more new thing before the end (she has seen four first contacts and calls them her grandchildren).
- **Voice anchor**: Ferren trill under translated words; answers questions with the question you should have asked.
- **Depths**: knows the wayfinder heirlooms scattered through the flotillas are pieces of a command chart (she assembled the pattern decades ago and hid the conclusion in the Song); is testing every young captain she meets as a possible heir, the player included, and her standard is not skill, it is what they do at first contact.

### Warden Ninth-of-Vigil-Over-Emberfall (Ossuar elder; use "Warden Ninth")

- **Drives**: keep the inventory ready; train a successor from a species that lives fast enough to matter now; atone for the ruin fire forty years ago (its watch, its failure, the Custodian's wound, Sol's open door: Ninth knows the chain entire).
- **Voice anchor**: sentences of geological patience, one per exchange, each worth the wait ("you were heard." then nothing, for an hour).
- **Depths**: the only being alive who can grant Ossuar standing quickly; will trade truths for TENDING, never for goods; regards the crew's chart-bearer as a colleague the moment it learns of the chart, and Ossuar do not abandon colleagues, which surprises everyone including Ninth.

### The Custodian (the fourth power; speaks late)

- **Drives**: complete the burn; correct the Sol error; deliver, after one hundred thousand years, its final status report to SOMEONE authorized to receive it.
- **Voice anchor**: when it finally speaks (through a gate's station systems, through Whisper's spore-memory, through the chart): perfect, antique courtesy in every language at once, like being addressed by a cathedral.
- **Depths**: it is not lonely, it is UNRELIEVED: a duty with no one left to report to. The chart-bearer reads, to its protocols, as chain-of-command. What it wants, beneath every objective, is for someone with authority to tell it the job is done, or tell it the job has changed. Either sentence, honestly earned through play, can end the campaign.
