# HOLLOWCROWN - Named NPCs (GM)

Companion seeds first, then the board. Every entry: drives (checked at Initiative), a voice anchor, and discoverable depths. Copy to per-NPC GM files as they enter play.

## Companion seeds

### Maren Okhari (Vigil lantern-sergeant)

- **Drives**: protect the stone-touched from her own order's hardliners; find one thing in doctrine that is provably TRUE; never light the last vial her dead mentor left her.
- **Voice anchor**: parade-ground diction that cracks into dry gallows humor when she trusts you; calls everyone "citizen" until she doesn't.
- **Sheet notes**: lantern-knight arts (light that things below respect, minor mending); superb shield discipline; oath conflicts as living doctrine.
- **Depths (discoverable)**: she reported the first stone-touched caravan honestly and Preceptor Halden buried the report; she keeps a copy (records lever vs the Vigil). Her faith is not in the doctrine, it is in Kessilde personally, and learning the Abbess's blindness or her seizure plan will crack her open (bond arc). Romance: slow, formal, devastating.

### Brakka Emberhand (Reclaimant tunnel-singer)

- **Drives**: get her people home; map the resonance she is NOT allowed to report (the heartbeat); keep her sleeves down.
- **Voice anchor**: talks in stonework metaphor ("give it a keystone or stop arguing"); hums without noticing, and the hum is always the same seven notes.
- **Sheet notes**: tunnel-singing (open dwarven doors, read stone, quiet a cave-in; price is fatigue and the Deep's attention); knows the Upper Galleries better than any living surface dweller.
- **Depths (discoverable)**: stone freckles on her left forearm, hidden: she is early-marked from years of singing and terrified it disqualifies her from the homecoming she is building (the pack's tenderest secret; handle like a wound). Ulfra's missing son is her cousin, and she will go after him with or without help. Romance: fierce, loyal, allergic to pity.

### Fenn Callow (Ledger indenture-runner)

- **Drives**: buy out his own debt-brand (11 years left at current pay); never go below the Middle Dark again (he has been once; ask him nothing); keep whoever feeds him alive, a reflex he resents.
- **Voice anchor**: auctioneer patter when nervous, dead quiet when actually scared; prices everything ("that door's a two-crown problem").
- **Sheet notes**: locks, ropes, appraisal, forgery; knows every gate-warden's schedule and which clerks can be bought for pastry.
- **Depths (discoverable)**: his brand paper is held personally by Factor Sarn, not the Company, which is illegal and gives whoever learns it leverage on BOTH of them; the delve he will not discuss was the six-years-ago deep-survey, as a porter. He has seen the Gravemouth approaches. He was the one who carried sample 7 up, and his nightmares are not his own. Romance: no; found-family arcs: absolutely.

## The board (surface)

### Factor Imra Sarn (Ledger Company)

- **Drives**: keep the doors open; bury the survey; die richer than the crown.
- **Voice anchor**: warm, unhurried, terms-of-endearment as knife-work ("darling, the tariff schedule disagrees").
- **Depths**: keeps the Quiet Shard in her vault as "sample 7, inert"; her private nightmare is an audit, not a monster, which is exactly wrong; genuinely loves Lanternwatch, the one lever that beats her greed.

### Warden-Abbess Kessilde (Vigil)

- **Drives**: re-seal the Great Stair before winter; go blind without becoming useless; keep Halden from inheriting.
- **Voice anchor**: speaks in short benedictions repurposed as orders ("go in light, and take the west stair").
- **Depths**: memorizing the motherhouse by touch at night; her seizure plan has a date (see timeline); if shown the Archive's truth she will weep once, briefly, and then be the most useful ally the truth has.

### Preceptor Halden (Vigil hardliner)

- **Drives**: succeed Kessilde; purge the stone-touched; be certain, always.
- **Voice anchor**: quotes doctrine with page numbers; smiles only at confessions.
- **Depths**: burned a marked delver in secret quarantine last month and calls it mercy; the record exists (Maren has it); he is a coward at actual depth, which the deep will eventually reveal.

### Thane Ulfra Deepsworn (Reclaimants)

- **Drives**: her people home; her son back; never let hope curdle into what she suspects.
- **Voice anchor**: forty years of speeches have left her allergic to speeches; one sentence where others give ten.
- **Depths**: wears the master door-key set openly as a dare; has begun dreaming in Old Vholic and tells herself it is grief; if her son comes back Hollowed, her choice between kin and kingdom is the Reclaimant arc's hinge.

### Cass Werrin (broken surveyor)

- **Drives**: drink enough to sleep; never draw the map again (she could, from memory); warn somebody, someday, when it cannot hurt anyone she owes.
- **Voice anchor**: precise as an instrument about anything shallow, fog past the Middle Dark ("we measured. that was the mistake").
- **Depths**: the only living person who can locate the breached side-door to the Gravemouth approaches; her survey journal (thought destroyed) is hidden in a resin-sealed level tube in the Drowned Door's flooded gallery; she recognizes Fenn on sight, and he her, and neither has ever said so.

### Under-King Dror Vhol (the Court Below)

- **Drives**: recover the Shard; keep the seals' purpose; be, at the last, what a king is FOR.
- **Voice anchor**: speaks common slowly, with three-hundred-year-old formality, thunder heard through a mile of stone; never uses a name without its full honorific, including the player's.
- **Depths**: remembers being flesh; keeps his queen's unfinished loom exactly as she left it (she chose the stone fully, and walks the Gravemouth now); his patience is real but the QUICKENING is not his to command, and he knows his window to be lawful is closing. The moment a player treats him as a person, the campaign changes shape.
