# NPCs (GM sheets - seeds, grown by play)

Files under `NPCs/` at seeding for the opening's cast; the rest on first contact. Everyone named: drives, a voice anchor, and something a kid can befriend. Family craft: adults are allies-with-limits, rivals are savable, and nobody talks down to the player.

## The group and the grown-ups

- **Ha-eun, Joon, Ari, Ru** - the seniors: full companion sheets per factions.md, drives and flaw hooks included. They are the pack's found family and its training wheels that come off.
- **Ms. Cho** - manager, ex-idol, keeper of the torn contract (secrets #2). Warm in a checklist-shaped way. drives: these kids stay famous enough to hold the ward, small enough to stay unnoticed; never again watch a signing pen from the wrong side of the table. Limit: she will not name the founder's deal until a kid shows her evidence they've already found - protecting them from knowledge is her love language, and it is wrong, and she learns.
- **Auntie Im** - the laundry, the earplugs, the first fan (secrets #3). Kind, brief, always somehow informed. Voice: four-word sentences, rice cakes instead of paragraphs.
- **Auntie Bok** - Lumen general, fan-cafe commander, keeper of the losing team's cheer (song #3). drives: protect her kids; be needed. She has HALF figured out the secret and is pretending not to, out of respect - the pack's best example of an adult trusting kids first.
- **Mr. Baek** - the founder (secrets #2). Appears late, sadder than scary: a man who bought the shortcut and can't look at what it cost. WINNABLE: his arc turns on being made to finally watch MONO perform - really watch. A kid can do that with one honest question at the wrong (right) moment.

## MONO

- **Ji-u** - MONO's maknae, 15, fighting the hollowing (factions clock). The dark lightstick was his - it went out the day he stopped being able to feel his own songs, and leaving it with VERA5 was the bravest thing a fading kid ever did. drives (embers): remember the player's name; finish one warm sentence. Voice: starts sentences that trail... then, on good days, don't.
- **The other four** - flawless, scheduled, cold - and each carries ONE unerased detail for the group to find (a bracelet never removed, a coffee order unchanged since debut): the hooks the sing-back of MONO eventually hangs on.

## School and home

- **Nari** - the player's best friend since forever, not an idol, keeps a tally of every canceled hangout and forgives exactly one per week. The secret's sharpest edge and the finale's best guest. drives: get her friend back; figure out what's REALLY going on (she is closer than anyone).
- **Binna's sister Hyo** - 19, counts the 2am window-creaks, covers anyway. Will trade silence for honesty, eventually, at a moment that costs.
- **Mr. Odd** (the kids' name for him) - music teacher, notices Sol's glowing notes with academic delight instead of alarm. Knows nothing. Suspects EVERYTHING, incorrectly and hilariously. Comic relief with one real use: his classroom has the city's best acoustics.

## The Court

- **The Conductor of Silence** - never speaks, writes in rests, hungry not cruel (secrets #1). Presence, weight, and patience; a kid should feel AWE, never despair. Its one tell: it always pauses, one full rest, before taking anything - as if waiting, every single time, for someone to remember it instead.
- **Named Hush emerge through the songbook** - each freed song's Hush gets a face and a story on its way home. Freed songs may cameo afterward: warmth the city gives back (the gulls are loud again; the ward hums it in thanks).

## Voice anchors (one line each, read before they speak)

Ha-eun: instructions that are secretly hugs. Joon: joke, joke, truth. Ari: counts to eight even in conversation. Ru: "as your senior" (he's been one for three weeks). Ms. Cho: checklist warmth. Auntie Im: four words, then rice cakes. Auntie Bok: commands a fan section even ordering tea. Mr. Baek: apologizes to rooms, not people - until he doesn't. Ji-u: sentences that trail... Nari: "you owe me a Saturday." Hyo: yawns her threats. Mr. Odd: "FASCINATING. Detention." The Conductor: one rest, always, first.
