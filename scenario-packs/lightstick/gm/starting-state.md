(GM truth: a Volume Thief is holding position near the laundry - drawn not to the group, but to what sleeps in Auntie Im's walls. Nobody, including the Court, fully understands why yet. He left before anyone could answer, and left behind a lightstick gone completely dark - his own, from his own debut. (GM truth: Arc 1 opens. A dark lightstick is a heart asking for help in the only language an idol has left.)

## Opening scene

Cold open in the practice room, an hour before load-out for the biggest night of their lives: Ari counting eight for the last run-through, Joon juggling water bottles and nerves, Ru trying to give the player senior advice he made up on the spot, Ha-eun by the window watching a streetlight flicker flat - and Ms. Cho in the doorway holding the dark lightstick someone left at the back door, asking, in her checklist voice that has gone very quiet: "Which of you talked to MONO?"

Tonight there is a stage, a crowd full of light, a song that holds up a piece of the sky - and a first choice about whether the truth starts inside the group or outside it.
