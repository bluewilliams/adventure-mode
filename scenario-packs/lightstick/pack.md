# LIGHTSTICK

The first family pack for Adventure Mode. Haneul City, tonight, under the stage lights. Every real song holds the dark back a little. Your group just debuted, and the dark noticed.

## Player-safe brief (what a rookie idol knows by week two)

You debuted three weeks ago. VERA5: five members, one single ("Stay Loud"), a practice room above a laundry, and a manager, Ms. Cho, who watches the exits in every room like a bodyguard, which is strange for someone who says she used to be an accountant.

Then, after the first real concert, the four older members sat you down and told you the truth:

- **The songs are load-bearing.** Under Haneul City sleeps the Hush - things that eat sound and the feelings inside sound. A century ago the First Singer wove the Bright Chorus, a song so true it became a wall. Every REAL performance since - every song sung like it matters - re-weaves a thread of it.
- **Idol groups are the wardens.** Not just yours: every group in the city carries a piece of the watch, passed down from the First Singer's students. The stage is the ritual. The fans' joy is the fuel. Nobody outside can ever know, because fear is the one sound the Hush can drink straight.
- **You can hear them now.** Debut does something. Streetlights hum flat when a Hush is near. Your favorite song goes gray in your headphones. Somewhere in a crowd, one person's laugh just... stops. That's the hunt starting.
- **MONO is what losing looks like.** The city's most famous group: five perfect faces, chart-topping songs that all sound like winter. The seniors say MONO used to be warm. The seniors say don't get close. The seniors won't say what happened.
- **The rules of the watch**: hunt in pairs, never miss practice, never lie to the Lumen (the fandom - they saved the group before you joined, the story goes), and no matter what you see down there - the Hush are never for killing. Nobody has ever explained that last rule to you. The seniors go quiet when you ask.

Your life now: school by day, practice by evening, and some nights - the nights the streetlights hum - the five of you go out with lightsticks and learn what your songs can really do.

Character creation starts with three questions the GM will ask: which member you are (dancer, rapper, or vocalist - or describe your own), what sound you'd miss most if the Hush took it, and who in your normal life you most hate lying to.

## Pregens (jump in now)

Skip the interview and be playing in a minute; who they are emerges at the table.

- **Dae-ho ("Echo")**, main dancer, 15. Hears the Hush coming before anyone - a pressure behind the music only he feels, which is why his solos keep saving people who never know they were saved. Terrified of freezing onstage. Has never once frozen where it counted.
- **Binna ("Rhyme")**, rapper, 16. Fastest mouth in Haneul City and secretly so shy offstage she orders food by app to avoid phone calls. Her verses hit the Hush like thrown light. Her big sister is starting to notice the 2am window.
- **Sol**, vocalist, 15. When Sol holds a real note, it GLOWS - faintly, gold, and getting less faint every week, which is becoming a problem in music class. Keeps a notebook of melodies nobody taught her. Some of them are very old. She doesn't know where they come from. (The GM does.)

## Starting situation

See `gm/starting-state.md`. Friday. Tonight is VERA5's first music-show win - the biggest stage yet, a full Lumen crowd, the Light Meter's best night of the year. And this afternoon, three things happened: someone's notebook of made-up melodies hummed by itself in a school bag, the streetlights on the practice-room block started humming flat two hours before sundown - and MONO's youngest member, waiting alone by the laundry's back door, asked to speak to "the new one," looking over his shoulder the whole time, and left a lightstick that has gone completely dark.
