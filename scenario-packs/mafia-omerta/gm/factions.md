# OMERTA - Factions (GM)

## The Marchetti family

- **Who**: ~40 made men plus hundreds of associates. Boss Sal "the Anvil" Marchetti; underboss Nicky Tempesta; capos include Marco Santoro (South Side) and Joe "Joe Books" Abbatino (unions/pension skim). Lawyer: Adele Katz.
- **Agenda**: survive itself. Publicly: business as usual. Actually: a dying boss, an unworthy heir, federal pressure, and a drug ban half the next generation resents.
- **Doctrine**: the old rules, enforced unevenly. Discipline is Santoro; appetite is Nicky; the gap between them is where the pack lives.
- **Weaknesses (discoverable)**: the Bellavista back-room phone is tapped (technical/observation: the phone company "repair" visit is in the club's memory); Sal's illness (proximity/Adele/observation of the tremor); Nicky's heroin money (follow the Docks arc); the pension skim is an accounting paper-trail the IRS is already pulling (Joe Books' ledgers, discoverable via Adele or burglary).

## The Doyle crew

- **Who**: ~15 hard cases around **Brendan Doyle**, waterfront union muscle since his father's day. North docks, Local 1332, hijacking, and now (quietly) the Montreal heroin pipeline.
- **Agenda**: hold the docks, feed the pipeline, and never again take a knee to Italians - his father did, once, and Brendan was raised on the shame of it.
- **Doctrine**: tribal loyalty, sudden violence, long memory. The Doyles keep faith inside the crew absolutely and regard everyone else as weather.
- **Weaknesses (discoverable)**: Brendan's brother **Colm** is a violent liability whose next stupidity is always a week away (observation); the Montreal supplier despises Brendan and would deal past him with anyone stable (social, dangerous); union pension audit exposure ties them, absurdly, to the Marchetti skim - the two enemies share a federal problem (deep discovery, peace-or-war lever).

## Operation BRICKWALL (the task force)

- **Who**: FBI + IRS, eight agents, led by **Agent Dana Reyes** - first-generation, relentless, allergic to shortcuts, better at this than the family believes a woman can be in 1979 (their sexism is her best asset).
- **Agenda**: RICO the Marchetti administration to its knees. Not interested in associates except as rungs.
- **Doctrine**: patient, legal, cumulative. Reyes doesn't fabricate and doesn't bluff twice.
- **Weaknesses (discoverable)**: understaffed and time-boxed - Washington wants indictments this fiscal year, which is why Petey is being squeezed (his handler pressure is visible if watched); the wire's chain-of-custody has a defect a sharp lawyer could kill (Adele-tier discovery); Reyes herself keeps a private line open for people who want OUT of the life without wearing one - the pack's only honest exit door, and it looks exactly like the trap everyone believes it is.

## The Starlite Lounge (neutral ground, rumor tier)

- Denise Alvarez's bar sits ON the line between territories and has stayed neutral for eleven years because everyone needs one room where nobody dies. GM truth: Denise pays no one, is protected by no one, and has kept the peace with information triage - she defuses collisions before they happen by deciding who hears what, when. She knows more than any capo. Making her an ally is slow; making her an enemy is fatal in ways nobody can trace.
