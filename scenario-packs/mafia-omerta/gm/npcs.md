# OMERTA - NPC Canon (GM)

Starting contacts are pre-seeded: at game seed, create player-facing `NPCs/` notes for Petey, Santoro, Denise, Frank Bell, and Uncle Gio containing ONLY their brief-level facts. Everything below flagged `known: false` stays here until earned.

## Petey Ranucci - the Friend

- Made two years ago, your childhood friend and vouch. Companion, bond starts **warm**. `known: starts-true`
- **Flipped by Operation BRICKWALL five weeks ago** (cigarettes + gun charge; handler Agent Reyes). Stalling, has not given the player. See secrets.md for tells and resolution space. `known: false`
- **Weakness**: his mother's mortgage is what the family "fixed" when he got made; the FBI knows it and so does Santoro. Both sides hold the same string. `known: false`
- Traits: funny under pressure, sentimental, physically brave and morally exhausted; lies badly to people he loves.

## Marco "the Saint" Santoro - the Capo

- Capo, South Side crew, Bellavista Social Club. Quiet, devout, never raises his voice. Bond-capable (mentor-shaped, glacial trust). `known: starts-true`
- Sal's private pick for the crown; does not want it and has told no one but his priest. `known: false`
- **Weakness**: his faith is real - there are orders he will slow-walk if the target is genuinely innocent, and a pattern-reader can map his exceptions. `known: false`
- **Weakness**: he suspects there is a rat and is quietly auditing his own crew; he is one bad coincidence from suspecting Petey. `known: false`
- Traits: tests people with silence; pays loyalty back with protection; remembers every favor in both directions.

## Sal "the Anvil" Marchetti - the Boss

- Boss since 1949. Public: iron. `known: starts-true` (public shape only)
- Dying (Parkinson's, concealed). Favors Santoro over Nicky. See secrets. `known: false`
- **Weakness**: vanity about the concealment; he will destroy anyone who names his illness before he does, even usefully. `known: false`
- Traits: speaks in questions; sentimental about the neighborhood he strip-mined; keeps his own counsel and Adele's.

## Nicky Tempesta - the Heir Apparent

- Underboss. Loud suits, louder confidence, real muscle behind both. `known: starts-true` (public)
- In the heroin pipeline with the Doyles' Montreal supplier, against Sal's ban. `known: false`
- **Weakness**: gambling debts to a Philadelphia book that he services with drug money - a paper trail with two lit ends. `known: false`
- **Weakness**: talks business on the Bellavista back-room phone. The tapped one. `known: false`
- Traits: generous when flush, vicious when embarrassed; mistakes fear for respect; genuinely loves his terrible nephews.

## Denise Alvarez - the Starlite

- Runs the Starlite Lounge, neutral ground. Bond-capable, romance-capable (slow, eyes-open, zero interest in being anyone's moll; she falls for judgment, not danger, and will end it the day the player brings the life through her door). `known: starts-true` (bar and discretion)
- The neutrality mechanism (information triage, protected by nobody). `known: false`
- **Weakness**: a younger brother, Ray, two years into a five-year bit at Rahway; his safety inside is her one lever, and three factions could reach him. `known: false`
- Traits: dry, exact, kind in unmarked bills; forgets nothing.

## Frank Bell - the Toll Booth

- Patrol cop on Santoro's payroll. `known: starts-true`
- Takes from BOTH sides of the line: also on a Doyle envelope. Not an informant, just greedy; the ambiguity should read as rat-tells to a paranoid player (authored red herring). `known: false`
- **Weakness**: his brother-in-law owes the Marchetti book eleven grand; Frank's been quietly eating the vig, which is why he always needs more. `known: false`
- Traits: friendly, transactional, loyal to his pension.

## Uncle Gio - the Shelf

- Your uncle; shelved decades ago, silent about why; Sunday dinners. Bond starts **devoted** (family). `known: starts-true`
- The 1961 refusal, the debt of respect, the buried informant of the old era. See secrets. `known: false` (deepest single unlock in the pack)
- **Weakness**: the player. He buried his own life to keep hands clean of a friend's blood; watching his sister's kid climb the same ladder is the one thing that can make him talk. `known: false`
- Traits: cooks like an apology; changes the subject with food; watches the player's shoes to see how the money is coming.

## Agent Dana Reyes - the Task Force

- Exists to the player only as "federal money in town" at start. `known: false` until BRICKWALL surfaces
- Runs Petey; runs the wire; keeps the honest exit door open. See factions. `known: false`
- Traits: never bluffs twice; keeps every promise, which the family would recognize as their own oldest rule if they ever looked at her straight.

## Adele Katz - the Counsel

- The family's lawyer of record. `known: starts-true` (name only)
- Consigliere in all but title; holds the diagnosis, the will, the pension-skim exposure, and the wire's chain-of-custody defect. The most leveraged single mind in Brickport. `known: false`
- Bond-capable (respect only, earned in competence and discretion; she has watched charm try to work on her for forty years).
