# OMERTA

A crime-family ascension pack for Adventure Mode. Brickport, New Jersey, 1979. You are nobody. That can change.

## Manifest

## Player-safe brief (what an associate knows)

Brickport: a port city sliding downhill between New York and Philadelphia, container cranes rusting, unions strong, cops cheap, everything a little bent. The Marchetti family has run the South Side since Prohibition: gambling, loansharking, the docks, the unions, cigarettes, and anything that falls off a truck.

You grew up knowing the rules the way you know weather:

- **The family has a shape.** Sal "the Anvil" Marchetti has been boss for thirty years. Nicky Tempesta is underboss. Crews answer to capos; you answer to **Marco "the Saint" Santoro**, capo of the South Side crew, out of the Bellavista Social Club on Adler Ave.
- **The rules are the rules.** You kick up. You keep your mouth shut. You don't touch drugs (officially). You don't raise your hand to a made man. Nothing happens to a made man without a sit-down.
- **Made is the door.** Until your button, you are spendable. Getting made takes earning, a clean vouch, and Italian blood by the old rules - rules people whisper about bending for the right earner.
- **The Doyles hold the North docks.** Irish crew, waterfront local, mean as winter. There is a line down the middle of Brickport and everyone knows which bars sit on which side.
- **The law is bought, not gone.** Patrol cops take envelopes. Detectives take bigger ones. But there's federal money in town lately, and nobody knows whose phones are warm.

### Your starting contacts (pre-seeded, you know these people)

- **Petey Ranucci** - your oldest friend, made two years ago, the reason anyone lets you in the room. Vouched for you. Something's been off with him for a month.
- **Marco "the Saint" Santoro** - your capo. Quiet, devout, terrifying in a way that never raises its voice. Decides what you're worth.
- **Denise Alvarez** - runs the bar at the Starlite Lounge. Hears everything, repeats nothing, owes nobody. Your favorite person in Brickport.
- **Frank Bell** - patrol cop on Santoro's payroll. Friendly the way a toll booth is friendly.
- **Uncle Gio** - your mother's brother. Was somebody in this thing once; got shelved years ago and won't say why. Cooks Sunday dinner like an apology.

Character creation starts with three questions the GM will ask: how you're connected, what you bring that the family wants, and what line you won't cross (and what you've already done).

## Pregens (jump in now)

Skip the interview and be playing in a minute; who they are emerges at the table. Or build your own from the creation questions.

- **Nicky Averona**, bookkeeper's nephew. Grew up running numbers slips; can read a ledger like scripture and lie to anyone but his mother. The family thinks he is soft. He is not soft; he is patient.
- **Frank Della Rosa**, dock loader with debts. Strong back, quick temper, three weeks behind to a shylock, and one favor owed to him by exactly the wrong person. Wants one big year and out; nobody gets one big year and out.
- **Carla Moretti**, cocktail waitress at the Starlight Room. Hears everything, forgets nothing, banned from the casino floor in Atlantic City under a different name. Saving for a bar of her own with money that keeps needing to be lent.
