# WHITEOUT - NPC Canon (GM)

Facts are flagged `known: false` until earned in play; on discovery, flip the flag and write the fact through to the game's player-facing `NPCs/` note. Bond-capable NPCs are marked; romance follows GAME.md bond rules.

## Dane Kittredge - the Tollman

- Former staff sergeant, MN National Guard. Held the Virginia checkpoint three days past orders; watched it fall anyway. `known: false`
- Rules ~30 armed survivors through predictable brutality and a medical-supply payroll. `known: false` (payroll), `known: starts-true` (armed, taxes roads: common knowledge)
- **Weakness**: the insulin/antibiotic pallet is nearly spent; his authority has a fuel gauge. `known: false`
- **Weakness**: keeps the checkpoint's final casualty list folded in his coat; treats it like a debt. A player who names the right dead man can reach the man inside the sergeant. `known: false`
- Traits: keeps every promise and every threat; despises waste; secretly relieved when someone stands up to him competently.

## Elias Voss - the Shepherd

- Hospice chaplain from the Duluth convoys. Warm voice, tired eyes, remembers every name and every sin you have told him. Bond-capable (trust ladder only; he romances no one, he collects people).
- Publicly: sanctuary-for-all. `known: starts-true` (the singing hill church is common knowledge)
- Actually: selection doctrine dressed as discernment; confession ledger as leverage. `known: false`
- **Weakness**: diabetic, insulin-dependent, supplied through a deniable arrangement with the Tollmen. `known: false` (the pack's deepest secret; two-faction discovery)
- **Weakness**: Corrine Ash, his first exile, is alive on the north shore and can testify. `known: false`
- Traits: never lies when a misleading truth will do; genuinely believes he is saving people; afraid of dying unwitnessed.

## Marta Kettunen - the Yard

- 61, scrapyard matriarch, third-generation. Deals straight, trusts slow. Bond-capable (hard-won; the Yard's respect is the pack's best "earned home" arc).
- **Weakness**: grandson Oskari, 15, is sneaking out to trade with the Tollmen and is being groomed as an informant. Marta doesn't know. Handling this well (or badly) is the fastest bond swing in the pack. `known: false`
- Traits: pays debts in kind, not words; sentimental only about machinery; decided long ago she will not survive her grandson and acts accordingly.

## Ruth Okafor - the Voice

- 44, former station engineer at KQIL, broadcasting survivor/dead name-lists nightly from the Birch Ridge transmitter shack. Alone. Bond-capable, romance-capable (slow, guarded, radio-first: players tend to fall for the voice before the person, and Ruth knows the power and loneliness of that).
- Generator has ~3 weeks of fuel. `known: false` (the countdown behind the lighthouse)
- Keeps THE notebook: every confirmed survivor and death in the region. Three factions want it; she has burned pages rather than let names become leverage before. `known: false`
- **Weakness**: her sister was in the Duluth convoy overrun on Highway 53; she reads the frozen horde's names on air without knowing one of them is walking in it. `known: false` (devastating late-arc reveal; handle with care)
- Traits: precise, dry-funny, incorruptible about the list; terrified of silence.

## Doc Halloran - the Needle

- 58, large-animal veterinarian, Iron Lake's only "doctor." Drinks exactly two fingers a night, no more, and hates that people need him too much for him to stop entirely.
- Suspects amputation can beat a fresh bite; has never had the nerve or a willing patient. `known: false` (flips the infection meta if the player learns and tests it)
- Treats anyone, any faction, no questions: which makes him the only person who knows roughly what medical supplies every faction holds. `known: false`
- Bond-capable (mentor-shaped).

## Corrine Ash - the Exile

- 37, elementary teacher, Voss's first "discernment" exile. Survived the ice-fishing shacks on rage and walleye. Missing two toes. Bond-capable, romance-capable (prickly, funny, zero patience for saviors; warms to competence and honesty, not rescue).
- Can testify to what the Congregation really is. `known: false` (she IS the weakness; keeping her alive is the arc)
- Carries Voss's spare ledger key stolen on her way out, and hasn't decided what she's willing to become to use it. `known: false`

## Dr. Lena Marsh - the Quiet House

- 71, retired wildlife biologist. Understands the thaw. Shoots trespassers. See factions. Bond-capable only through demonstrated scientific usefulness or genuine gentleness; immune to charm.
- Research state: has proven reanimation-after-thaw in specimens; has NOT yet extrapolated to the Highway 53 horde because she doesn't know its size. The player may be the one to complete the horrifying math. `known: false`
